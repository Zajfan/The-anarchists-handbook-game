The Situation
You are nobody.
No job. No future.
No illusions left about the system
that made you that way.
You found the Handbook in a dumpster
behind a copy shop —
water-damaged, half the pages stuck
together, but it was all there.
You read it cover to cover
on a park bench in the rain.

You can't un-read it.
The corporations own the buildings.
The State owns the cops.
The surveillance grid owns the streets.

And you — you've got the Handbook
and enough anger to do something.

Where do you start?
